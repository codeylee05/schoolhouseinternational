Welcome to School House

International Qualifications

Students studying through School House will be able to obtain international GCSE to A Level qualifications through any acccredited Cambridge exam center. These qualifications are accepted at prestigious universities globally.

Specialist Teachers

We continously train our teachers to act with creativity and empathy when facilitating our project based curriculum. Teaching children new concepts with deep understanding and hands on exploration.

Student-Parent Communication

We believe in the power of a collaborative relationship between students, teachers, and parents. We focus on using constructive feedback from our teachers and parents and reporting to engage students by assessing their study skills.

Inlcusive Enviroment

Traditional learning enviroments do not cater for neurodiverse children hence do we make it our mission to create a space where children cultivate a love for learning. We accomodate different learning styles accordingly.

Our Campuses

Exploratorium Nursery School

Catering for children aged from 4 weeks through to Grade R. Meals offered on campus. Open from Mon - Friday 06h30 - 18h00

School House Primary Campus

Catering for children from Grade 1 through to Grade 6. Light meals offered on campus. Open from 06h30 - 15h30. Aftercare options available until 18h00.

School House Seniors Campus

Equipped for our High school students doing their IGCSE, AS Level and GED qualifications. Campus includes a variety of clubs, science facilities, and extra mural activities
